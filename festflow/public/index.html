How FestFlow Works

Three phases, zero human firefighting.

📋

Phase 1 — Registration

Participants register for sessions. Volunteers submit skills and availability. AI maps them to the best-fit slots.

🔄

Phase 2 — Live Rescheduling

Someone drops out? AI detects the gap, scans the waitlist, picks a replacement, and notifies everyone automatically.

📨

Phase 3 — Personalised Briefings

Every volunteer receives an AI-crafted personal briefing email before the event — role, venue, timing, and encouragement.
