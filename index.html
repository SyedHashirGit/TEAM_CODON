FestFlow automates volunteer coordination, real-time rescheduling, and personalised briefings so coordinators can focus on the fest — not the firefighting.

How FestFlow Works

Three phases, zero human firefighting. The AI handles it all.

📋

Phase 1 — Registration

Participants register for sessions. Volunteers submit their skills and availability. AI immediately maps them to the best-fit event slots.

🔄

Phase 2 — Live Rescheduling

Someone drops out? The AI detects the gap, scans the waitlist, reasons over skill-matches, picks a replacement, and notifies everyone. No human needed.

📨

Phase 3 — Personalised Briefings

Every volunteer receives an AI-crafted personal briefing before the event — role, venue, timing, and encouragement. All automated.
